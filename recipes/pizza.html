Pizza Hut Classic Pizza

←Back

Ingrediants

  • 1 package of dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon suger
  • 1/2 teas salt
  • 1 1/3 cup warm milk(105°F)
  • 2 teas olive oil
  • 4 cups flour
  • 8oz tomato sauce
  • 1 teas dried oregano
  • 1/2 teas dried majoram
  • 1/2 teas dried basil
  • 1/2 teas garlic salt
  • 9oz vegitable oil
  • cooking spray
  • mozzerella cheese

Assembaly

  1. Mix the yeast, suger, salt, and milk in a mixing bowl until the yeast starts the bubble, then add the oil
  2. Add the flower in 1 cup increments and mix(if the dough is too thick add 1/2 cup of water)
  3. Turn the dough onto a lightly flowered surface and knead for 10 min
  4. Divide into 3 parts
  5. spread 3 ounces of oil into a 9 inch cake pan or cast iron skillit
  6. flaten the dough into 9-inch circles and place into the pans
  7. Lightly coat each dough disk wity cooking spray or oil and cover with plastic wrap. Place in a warm, draft-free location and let it rise for 1 to 1 1/2 hrs or until puffy
  8. combine the tomato sauce, oregano, majoram, basil, and garlic salt in a bowl and mix, and let sit for 1 hr at room temperature
  9. preheat the oven to 475°F
  10. spread 1/3 suace on each pizza and sprinkle with cheese
  11. add toppings of choice (if any) and cook for 11-15 min
  12. Enjoy!
