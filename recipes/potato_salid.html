Potato Salad

←Back

This recipe is very flexible so you can change the amount of ingrediants to your preferances and even add other ingrediants

Ingrediants

  • potatos(4-6 depending on the size)
  • 5 hardboiled eggs
  • Mayo
  • Bacon
  • salt
  • pepper

Assembaly

  1. Cook the potatos by Boiling water in a pot and cook for an hour (You can check whether of not their done by sticking a knife in them and if they're soft, then they are done)
  2. Boil water in a seperate pot and cook the eggs for 20min
  3. Cook the bacon at 400Â°F and cook for 20-25 min(watch closely! bacon cooks fast)
  4. When the potatos are done cooking, peel them and dice them up into 1/2 inch sized chunks
  5. When the eggs are done, let them cool for 5-10 min and then peel and dice them up
  6. mix the potatos, eggs and crumble up the bacon into a bowl
  7. add mayo to your preferance(I like enough to add a thin coat to all the ingrediants)
  8. sprinkle in the salt and pepper and mix
  9. let sit in the fridge for an hour and enjoy!
